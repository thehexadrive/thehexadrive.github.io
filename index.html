HexaDrive — To a safer world
HexaDrive
In modern-times, owing to hectic schedules it becomes very difficult to remain active all the time. Often , people fall asleep on the wheel which can lead to serious consequences, there can be accidents and many may even lose their lives. This situation is much more common than we know and hence, it is very important to counter this problem. So to address this issue, we have come up with a solution, Hexadrive.

ABOUT THE PRODUCT

SOLAR POWERED

The HexaDrive is primarily powered on solar energy using photovoltaic cells, making it both consumer and environment friendly.

USES BIOPLASTIC

Focusing on our environment friendly techniques, we have introduced bioplastic as the structural material for our products.

PREVENTS ACCIDENTS

It is estimated that upto 40% of car accidents are due to sleepy drivers. HexaDrive prevents exactly that.

SAFER COMMUNITY

Our goal is driving towards a safer future. HexaDrive builds a safer community for drivers, pedestrians and cyclists.

EASY INSTALLATION

The installation of the HexaDrive can be done in under 5 minutes. No tools or skill required.

LESS BATTERY NEEDED

Being an environmentally friendly product, the HexaDrive rarely needs to be powered on batteries and can even be used without them.

UNIQUE DESIGN

The HexaDrive has a compact and unique hexagonal design which allows it to be placed on the dashboard without obstructing the driver's view.

STRUCTURE AND DESIGN

The HexaDrive works on the basis of a program that identifies whether the driver is alert or dozing using a camera. On the basis of it’s observation it starts giving out a buzzing alarm which automatically stops when the driver is alert once more. It even has a compact and unique hexagonal design which allows it to be placed on the dashboard without obstructing the driver's view.
